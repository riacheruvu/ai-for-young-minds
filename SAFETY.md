# Safety and Privacy

This resource does not collect, transmit, or store information. Text entered into the activities remains in the browser page and disappears when the page is refreshed.

## If using an external AI tool

Do not enter a child’s:

- Full name
- Face or identifiable image
- Home or school address
- Precise location
- Passwords or account details
- Health information
- Private messages
- Information about friends or family

An adult should review the privacy settings, age requirements, and terms of any external AI service before a child uses it.

## Facilitation

- Treat surprising AI output as something to inspect, not automatically trust.
- Avoid asking children to disclose sensitive experiences for the sake of an activity.
- Allow drawing, speaking, or offline role-play instead of typing.
- Stop an activity if it becomes upsetting, invasive, or developmentally unsuitable.
- Remind learners that an AI response can sound confident while being incomplete or wrong.

## Scope

This is an educational resource, not legal, medical, mental-health, or safety advice.
